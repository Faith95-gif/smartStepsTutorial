SIMPLE AND COMPOUND INTEREST

🎯 Introduction

Interest is the extra money paid for borrowing or earned for lending money. Think of it as "rent" paid for using someone else's money. Understanding simple and compound interest is essential for JAMB mathematics and real-life financial decisions like bank loans, savings accounts, and investments.

💰 Simple Interest

Simple Interest (SI) is calculated only on the original amount (principal) for the entire period.

SI = P × R × T100
Amount = Principal + Simple Interest

Where:

  • P = Principal (original amount)
  • R = Rate per annum (% per year)
  • T = Time (in years)

🔄 Compound Interest

Compound Interest (CI) is calculated on the principal plus accumulated interest from previous periods.

A = P(1 + R100)T
CI = Amount - Principal

📝 JAMB Past Questions with Solutions

Question 1 JAMB 2023

Find the simple interest on ₦5,000 at 8% per annum for 3 years.

Solution:

Given: P = ₦5,000, R = 8%, T = 3 years

SI = P × R × T100
SI = 5,000 × 8 × 3100
SI = 120,000100
SI = ₦1,200

Question 2 JAMB 2022

What principal will amount to ₦8,800 at 10% simple interest in 4 years?

Solution:

Given: Amount = ₦8,800, R = 10%, T = 4 years

Amount = P + SI = P + P × R × T100
8,800 = P + P × 10 × 4100
8,800 = P + 40P100
8,800 = P + 0.4P = 1.4P
P = 8,8001.4
P = ₦6,286 (to nearest naira)

Question 3 JAMB 2021

Find the compound interest on ₦4,000 at 5% per annum for 2 years.

Solution:

Given: P = ₦4,000, R = 5%, T = 2 years

A = P(1 + R100)T
A = 4,000(1 + 5100)2
A = 4,000(1.05)2
A = 4,000 × 1.1025
A = ₦4,410
CI = A - P = 4,410 - 4,000
CI = ₦410

Question 4 JAMB 2020

At what rate per annum will ₦3,600 amount to ₦4,320 in 2 years at simple interest?

Solution:

Given: P = ₦3,600, A = ₦4,320, T = 2 years

SI = A - P = 4,320 - 3,600 = ₦720
SI = P × R × T100
720 = 3,600 × R × 2100
720 = 7,200R100
720 = 72R
R = 72072
R = 10%

Question 5 JAMB 2019

Find the difference between compound interest and simple interest on ₦8,000 at 6% for 3 years.

Solution:

Given: P = ₦8,000, R = 6%, T = 3 years

Step 1: Calculate Simple Interest

SI = 8,000 × 6 × 3100 = 144,000100 = ₦1,440

Step 2: Calculate Compound Interest

A = 8,000(1 + 6100)3 = 8,000(1.06)3
A = 8,000 × 1.191016 = ₦9,528.13
CI = 9,528.13 - 8,000 = ₦1,528.13

Step 3: Find the Difference

Difference = CI - SI = 1,528.13 - 1,440
Difference = ₦88.13

💡 Key Differences and Memory Tips

Simple vs Compound Interest

Simple: Interest calculated only on principal (original amount)
Compound: Interest calculated on principal + accumulated interest

Quick Formula Recall

SI Formula: PRT100
CI Formula: P(1 + R100)T - P

When CI > SI

Compound interest is always greater than simple interest for the same principal, rate, and time period (when T > 1 year).
