Question 1 JAMB 2019

Simplify: 3n-1 √ó 27n+1 81n

Solution:

Step 1: Express all terms in base 3

27 = 33, so 27n+1 = (33)n+1 = 33(n+1) = 33n+3
81 = 34, so 81n = (34)n = 34n

Step 2: Substitute and simplify

3n-1 √ó 33n+3 34n
= 3n-1 √ó 3(3n+3)-4n
= 3n-1 √ó 33n+3-4n
= 3n-1 √ó 3-n+3
= 3(n-1)+(-n+3)
= 3n-1-n+3
= 32 = 9
