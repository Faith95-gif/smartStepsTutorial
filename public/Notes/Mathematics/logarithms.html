üí° Memory Tips for JAMB Success

The Fundamental Relationship

Always remember: If ax = N, then logaN = x
Logarithms and exponentials are inverse operations!

Special Values to Remember

logaa = 1 (any number to the power 1 is itself)
loga1 = 0 (any number to the power 0 is 1)

Product becomes Sum

When you see multiplication inside a log, you can split it:
log(AB) = log(A) + log(B)

Power comes Down

When you see a power inside a log, bring it to the front:
log(An) = n √ó log(A)
